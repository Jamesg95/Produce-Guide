Our Story

For almost one-hundred years, the Grigoras family has been growing, cultivating, and sharing their quality produce. It all started with a man - an immigrant from El Salvador - with a dream of putting down roots, both with his family and with his produce. James owned just a small plot of land and just a few corn seeds. With ambition, hard work, and the unrelenting support of his community, he was able to achieve that dream. Today, J. Grigoras Farms is the most well-renowned farm in the area and grows over 20 different types of fruits and vegetables.
